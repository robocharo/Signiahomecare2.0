About Signia Home Care

A Minnesota home care provider built on compassion, respect, and a deep commitment to the people and communities we serve.

Our Story

Care That Starts with People, Not Paperwork

Signia Home Care was founded with a simple but powerful belief: that every person deserves to receive care that is kind, skilled, and centered on who they truly are — not just a diagnosis or a checklist of needs.

We understand, firsthand, how overwhelming it can be to navigate the world of home care — for individuals who need support, and for the families who love them. We've seen how the right caregiver, at the right time, can change everything. That's the difference we set out to make.

Today, Signia Home Care is a trusted, state-certified home care provider serving Minneapolis and the broader Twin Cities area. More than our credentials, we're defined by our values: compassion, reliability, dignity, and community.

We are proud to serve individuals and families who trust us with some of life's most meaningful moments — and we take that trust seriously, every single day.

Mission & Vision

What We Stand For

Our Mission

"To provide compassionate, individualized home care services that promote dignity, safety, and independence — empowering individuals to live their fullest lives in the place they call home."

Our Vision

"To be the most trusted home care partner in Minnesota — known for our warm, skilled, and people-first approach that sets the standard for what excellent care looks like at home."

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Everything We Do

Our values aren't words on a wall — they're the lens through which every care decision is made, every relationship is built, and every day of service is delivered.

💙

Compassion

We approach every person we serve with genuine kindness, empathy, and a sincere desire to make their life better. Compassion is not a strategy — it's who we are.

🌟

Dignity

Every person deserves to be treated with deep respect — regardless of their condition, age, or background. We protect and honor the dignity of every individual we serve, in every interaction.

🔒

Integrity

We do what we say we'll do. We are honest, transparent, and accountable — to our clients, their families, and the broader community that trusts us with their care.

🎯

Excellence

We hold ourselves to high standards — in our clinical practices, our customer service, and in the continuous improvement of our programs. Good enough is never good enough.

🤝

Partnership

Great home care is a team effort. We collaborate closely with families, physicians, case managers, and community partners — because the best outcomes happen together.

🌱

Independence

We believe in people — their strength, their resilience, and their right to make choices about their own lives. Our care supports independence rather than replacing it.

What Sets Us Apart

Why Signia Home Care Is Different

There are many home care providers in Minnesota. We believe what makes Signia Home Care genuinely different isn't just our credentials or our service list — it's how we deliver care and who we are in the lives of the people we serve.

We take the time to truly get to know each individual — their history, their preferences, their goals, and what brings them joy. We don't treat people like cases. We treat them like neighbors, because in many ways, that's exactly what we are.

We also recognize the enormous role that family caregivers play. We listen to families, keep them informed, and work in partnership with them — because we know that supporting the whole family means better care for everyone involved.

🏅 State Certified — Two Programs Under One Roof

We hold a Minnesota Comprehensive Home Care license and a 245D designation — so we can provide both professional home care (including private duty nursing) and daily living support, helping with a wider range of needs than many other providers.

🌍 Culturally Responsive Care

Minneapolis is a wonderfully diverse community. We strive to provide care that is sensitive to the cultural backgrounds, languages, and traditions of the individuals we serve.

📞 Responsive Communication

We respond promptly to referrals, questions, and concerns. Families and care partners deserve to feel heard and supported — not stuck on hold or chasing down answers.

Community

Committed to the Twin Cities Community

Signia Home Care is more than a business — we're a community partner. We believe that access to quality home care makes our entire community stronger, healthier, and more connected.

We serve individuals and families in Minneapolis, Plymouth, Edina, Bloomington, St. Louis Park, Richfield, Eden Prairie, Minnetonka, and communities throughout the Twin Cities metro. We're proud to be part of the fabric of these neighborhoods — and deeply committed to the well-being of the people who live in them.

Whether you're a family looking for support, a case manager seeking a reliable partner, or a community organization wanting to connect residents with care — we are here, ready to help.
