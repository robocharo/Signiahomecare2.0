What Is Comprehensive Home Care?

A Minnesota Comprehensive Home Care license lets us provide the services of licensed health professionals — registered and practical nurses, therapists, dietitians, and social workers — alongside personal care, medication management, and household help. That includes private duty (home care) nursing: continuous, one-on-one RN or LPN care for people with more complex needs.

Because we are licensed for comprehensive home care, we can support individuals with complex medical needs as well as those who simply need a helping hand at home. All services are delivered with a consistent commitment to dignity, safety, and quality of life.

Services are individualized and available based on each person's assessment, support plan, physician orders where required, and staff availability. Contact us to discuss which services may be right for you or your loved one.
