About These Services

What Are Basic 245D Services?

Basic 245D is a group of supportive services for individuals with disabilities who want to live independently in the community. Think of it as everyday help — companionship, household tasks, overnight safety, and caregiver relief — rather than medical or clinical care.

These services are often covered through Minnesota Medicaid waiver programs. A case manager typically helps set up the support plan. If you're not sure where to start, we can help point you in the right direction — just give us a call.

Signia Home Care is state-certified to provide these services, and we deliver them with the same warmth, reliability, and respect that defines everything we do.

245D services require program eligibility and an individualized support plan. Services may vary based on waiver program, funding authorization, and individual assessment. Please contact us or your case manager to learn more.
