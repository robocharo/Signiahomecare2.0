Who We Are

A Minnesota Home Care Team That Truly Cares

At Signia Home Care, we believe that everyone deserves to live with comfort, dignity, and independence — no matter their age, ability, or circumstance. We are a licensed Minnesota home care provider serving Minneapolis and surrounding Twin Cities communities.

Our team provides a wide range of services — from skilled nursing and therapy to personal care, companionship, and help around the house. Whether you're recovering from an illness, managing a health condition, or simply need a helping hand at home, we're here for you.

We are certified by the State of Minnesota to provide both skilled home care and daily living support, so we can help with a broad range of needs — all under one roof.

Our Approach

Compassionate Care at Home — The Way It Should Be

We know that inviting a caregiver into your home is an act of trust. That's why everything we do is built around earning and keeping that trust — through kindness, skill, consistency, and respect.

Our caregivers don't just complete tasks. They listen. They learn what makes each person comfortable. They notice the small things. And they show up, day after day, with genuine care for the individuals they serve.

Home is where healing happens best. It's where people feel most themselves — surrounded by familiar things, routines, and the people they love. We are honored to be part of that environment and to support families in keeping their loved ones safe and well at home.

  • Skilled, trained, and compassionate caregivers
  • Individualized care plans built around your needs
  • Clear communication with families and healthcare teams
  • Unwavering commitment to safety and dignity
